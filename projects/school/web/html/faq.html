FAQ

 
What is TARC Retro Games?

TARC Retro Games provides a platform to introduce classic games or retro games to people who are interested in those games, and make more people familiar with the old games, and also provide the games to those who want to play them. We enable customers to buy games that they like after browsing the game encyclopedia/catalogue to find out more about old games.

How can I register?

Click the register button. You will be redirected to a form where you have to fill out your personal details. Afterwards, click submit to become a member!

How can I buy items?

When browsing the products, click add to cart. Navigate to cart and click checkout. You will be redirected to the payment website where you could enter your credit card details.
